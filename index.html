About Us

Aagraha Investments

AAGRAHA INVESMENTS & FINANCING PVT. LTD., [Aagraha]

Aagraha is a closely held private Limited Company regd., under the Companies Act 2013.it automatically comes under supervision of the MCA (MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS) GOVERNMENT OF INDIA.

The aim of this company is to promote the economy of street vendors and to further develop their businesses. This company provides loans and encourages them in various ways. The company provides financial support to women in both urban and rural areas to enhance their financial livelihoods and to help them start new businesses.

The company was promoted by its Directors, Mr. V.NARESH KUMAR, Mrs.V.RAMYA & Mrs.K.SATHYAPRIYA both hailing from one Aagraha family. AAGRAHA is engaged in providing micro-loans under the Individual/SHG model, credit-linked insurance, at our member's doorstep on behalf of our lending partners.

The promoters are already founder directors in Welfare Organization for Multipurpose mass Awareness Network (WOMAN), an Erode based welfare Trust with social outlook for women welfare and empowerment.

Social Impact

Aagraha Investments

Our organization, in collaboration with the Makkal Yuva Kendra organization, provides social services in many districts, feeds the homeless, and provides training for the improvement of women's livelihood. Our organization is working with many social concerns for the people.
