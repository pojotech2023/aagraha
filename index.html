About Us

Aagraha

AAGRAHA INVESMENTS & FINANCING PVT. LTD., [Aagraha]

AAGRAHA is a closely held Private Limited Company regd., under the Companies Act 2013. This company is registered at Registrar of Companies (ROC), RoC-Coimbatore. Incorporated with MCA on 28/11/2024. AAGRAHA INVESMENTS & FINANCING PRIVATE LIMITED having CIN U64990TZ2024PTC033049 & GSTIN 33ABBCA7354G1ZV is listed in the class of Private Company and classified as Non-Govt Company it automatically comes under supervision of the MCA (MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS) GOVERNMENT OF INDIA.

The company was promoted by its Directors,Mr. V.NARESH KUMAR, Mrs.V.RAMYA & Mrs.K.SATHYAPRIYA are hailing from one Aagraha family. AAGRAHA is engaged in providing micro-loans under the Individual/SHG model, credit-linked insurance, at our member's doorstep on behalf of our lending partners.

The aim of this company is to promote the economy of street vendors and to further develop their businesses. This company provides loans and encourages them in various ways. The company provides financial support to women in both urban and rural areas to enhance their financial livelihoods and to help them start new businesses. We adopt a joint liability group model to provide unsecured loans to our members. This model ensures credit discipline through Daily/Weekly/Bi-Weekly/Monthly installments. This allows you to repay your debt in sync with your accounts receivables.

The promoters are already founder directors in Welfare Organization for Multipurpose mass Awareness Network (WOMAN), an Erode based welfare Trust with social outlook for women welfare and empowerment.
